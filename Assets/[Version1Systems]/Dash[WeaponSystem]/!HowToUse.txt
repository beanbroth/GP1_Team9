1) Make a cool weapon as a prefab. It should work as a child of the player
2) Check out the weapon prefabs in "WeaponPrefabs" for referance 
3) Make some weapon variations! 
4) Make a scriptable object by rightlicking -> Create -> WeaponSystem -> WeaponData
5) Add a name to your scriptable obejct
6) Add your weapon upgrade prefabs to the scriptable object
7) Add the scriptable object to the "WeaponInvintory" scriptable object in "AllWeapons"
8) Good to go!!! Add your weapon unlocked weapons to test it!!
